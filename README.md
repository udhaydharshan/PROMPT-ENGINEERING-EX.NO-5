1. Introduction

Prompting plays a crucial role in determining the quality of outputs generated by AI tools such as ChatGPT. A prompt is the input or instruction given to an AI model to guide its response. Well-structured prompts help the model understand the task clearly, resulting in more accurate, relevant, and detailed outputs.
This report defines two prompt types—Naïve Prompt and Basic Prompt—and experimentally evaluates their impact on ChatGPT’s performance across multiple scenarios.

2. Definition of the Two Prompt Types
2.1 Naïve Prompt

A naive prompt is a simple, short, and unstructured instruction. It provides minimal context and does not clearly specify expectations regarding format, depth, or constraints.

Characteristics:

Brief and vague

Lacks structure

Minimal context

May lead to incomplete or generic responses

Example:
“Write a story.”

2.2 Basic Prompt

A basic prompt is clear, detailed, and structured. It provides specific instructions, context, and expectations, guiding the AI model toward a focused and high-quality response.

Characteristics:

Clear and descriptive

Structured instructions

Specifies tone, format, or length

Produces more accurate and in-depth results

Example:
“Write a 150-word creative story for children about friendship between a dog and a cat, with a positive moral.”

3. Experimental Methodology
3.1 Objective

To compare ChatGPT’s responses to naïve prompts versus basic prompts across different task scenarios.

3.2 Selected Test Scenarios

Creative story generation

Answering a factual question

Summarizing a concept

Providing advice or recommendations

3.3 Procedure

For each scenario, a naïve prompt and a basic prompt were created.

Both prompts were input into ChatGPT.

The generated responses were recorded.

Outputs were evaluated based on:

Quality

Accuracy

Depth

4. Test Scenarios and Prompts
Scenario 1: Creative Story Generation

Naïve Prompt:
“Write a story.”

Basic Prompt:
“Write a 150-word creative story set in a futuristic city where robots help humans, highlighting the theme of cooperation.”

Scenario 2: Answering a Factual Question

Naïve Prompt:
“What is photosynthesis?”

Basic Prompt:
“Define photosynthesis in simple terms, explain its process, and mention its importance in sustaining life on Earth.”

Scenario 3: Summarizing a Concept

Naïve Prompt:
“Explain climate change.”

Basic Prompt:
“Summarize climate change in 120 words, covering its causes, effects, and possible solutions.”

Scenario 4: Providing Advice

Naïve Prompt:
“Give study tips.”

Basic Prompt:
“Provide five practical study tips for college students to improve concentration and exam performance.”

5. Comparison of ChatGPT Responses
Table: Naïve vs Basic Prompt Outputs
Scenario	Naïve Prompt Output	Basic Prompt Output
Creative Story	Short, generic story with no clear theme	Well-structured story with clear setting, theme, and conclusion
Factual Question	One-line definition, limited detail	Clear definition, process explanation, and importance
Concept Summary	Broad explanation, lacks focus	Concise, well-organized summary with key points
Advice	General tips, repetitive ideas	Specific, actionable, and clearly listed recommendations
6. Evaluation of Responses
6.1 Quality

Basic prompts consistently produced higher-quality responses. The outputs were better organized, clearer, and more relevant to the task. Naïve prompts often resulted in generic or shallow answers.

6.2 Accuracy

Accuracy improved with basic prompts because the model was guided to include specific information. Naïve prompts sometimes missed important details or provided overly simplified responses.

6.3 Depth

Basic prompts encouraged deeper explanations, examples, and structured outputs. Naïve prompts generally lacked depth and completeness.

7. Analysis and Observations

ChatGPT consistently performs better with basic prompts, especially for complex tasks such as explanations, summaries, and advice.

For simple factual questions, naïve prompts may still work reasonably well.

Creative and analytical tasks benefit significantly from structured prompts.

Prompt clarity directly impacts the usefulness and reliability of AI-generated content.

8. Summary of Findings

Clear and structured prompts result in better quality, accuracy, and depth.

Naïve prompts may be sufficient for very simple or direct queries.

Basic prompts are essential when detailed, well-organized, and goal-oriented responses are required.
